# 👶 Paediatrics Competencies

Core knowledge and skills expected at each level of training in paediatrics.

---

## Medical Student (Final Year)

### Knowledge
- [ ] Growth and developmental milestones — motor, speech, social
- [ ] Approach to the febrile child
- [ ] Recognising the seriously ill child — danger signs
- [ ] Approach to respiratory distress in children
- [ ] Common childhood infections — measles, chickenpox, meningitis
- [ ] Childhood immunisation schedule (South African EPI)
- [ ] Nutritional assessment — kwashiorkor vs marasmus
- [ ] Approach to diarrhoea and dehydration — IMCI
- [ ] Neonatal examination — normal findings
- [ ] Approach to the jaundiced neonate

### Clinical Skills
- [ ] Paediatric history taking (with parent/guardian)
- [ ] Paediatric general examination
- [ ] Respiratory examination in a child
- [ ] Weight, height, head circumference measurement and plotting
- [ ] Road-to-Health Booklet interpretation

---

## Intern

### Knowledge
- [ ] All medical student competencies above
- [ ] IMCI algorithm — sick young infant and child
- [ ] Fluid management in children — maintenance, deficit, replacement
- [ ] Paediatric drug dosing — weight-based calculations
- [ ] Febrile convulsions — diagnosis and management
- [ ] Bronchiolitis management
- [ ] Croup — severity assessment and management
- [ ] Asthma in children — acute management
- [ ] Neonatal resuscitation (NLS)
- [ ] Recognising child abuse — physical, sexual, neglect
- [ ] HIV in children — diagnosis, staging, ART initiation

### Clinical Skills
- [ ] Paediatric IV cannulation
- [ ] Intraosseous access (IO) — technique and indications
- [ ] Paediatric nasogastric tube insertion
- [ ] Lumbar puncture in a child (supervised)
- [ ] Bag-mask ventilation in a child
- [ ] Paediatric CPR

### Wits-Specific Protocols
- [ ] CHBAH paediatric fluid protocol
- [ ] Wits paediatric antibiotic guideline
- [ ] SA national ART guidelines for children

---

## Junior Doctor

### Knowledge
- [ ] All intern competencies above
- [ ] Paediatric emergency — anaphylaxis, status epilepticus, DKA
- [ ] Neonatal sepsis — risk factors, investigation, management
- [ ] Failure to thrive — investigation and management
- [ ] Chronic lung disease of prematurity
- [ ] Common paediatric surgical conditions — intussusception, pyloric stenosis
- [ ] Adolescent medicine — confidentiality, consent

---

!!! warning "Drug doses"
    Always calculate paediatric drug doses by weight. Use the [Paediatric Drug Dosing Quick Reference](ref-drug-dosing.md) and double-check with a senior before administering.
