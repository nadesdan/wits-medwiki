# 🔪 Surgery Competencies

Core knowledge and skills expected at each level of surgical training.

---

## Medical Student (Final Year)

### Knowledge
- [ ] Approach to the acute abdomen
- [ ] Differential diagnosis of right iliac fossa pain
- [ ] Bowel obstruction — causes, diagnosis, management
- [ ] Hernia types — inguinal, femoral, umbilical, incisional
- [ ] Principles of wound healing
- [ ] Surgical site infection — prevention and management
- [ ] Principles of informed consent for surgery
- [ ] Perioperative fluid management basics
- [ ] DVT prophylaxis in surgical patients
- [ ] Basic interpretation of surgical drains output

### Clinical Skills
- [ ] Abdominal examination
- [ ] Hernia examination
- [ ] Stoma examination
- [ ] Wound assessment and documentation
- [ ] Sterile technique and surgical scrub
- [ ] Simple interrupted suture

---

## Intern

### Knowledge
- [ ] All medical student competencies above
- [ ] Preoperative assessment and optimisation
- [ ] Antibiotic prophylaxis for common operations
- [ ] Postoperative analgesia — WHO ladder, PCA principles
- [ ] Postoperative complications — ileus, anastomotic leak, wound dehiscence
- [ ] Surgical drains — types, indications, management
- [ ] Nasogastric tube indications and management
- [ ] Stoma types and basic care
- [ ] Recognition of acute limb ischaemia
- [ ] Burns assessment — TBSA, fluid resuscitation (Parkland formula)

### Clinical Skills
- [ ] Surgical scrub and gowning
- [ ] Suturing — interrupted, continuous, mattress
- [ ] Wound closure and dressing
- [ ] Nasogastric tube insertion
- [ ] Urinary catheterisation
- [ ] Assisting in theatre — retraction, suction, diathermy safety
- [ ] Drain removal

### Wits-Specific Protocols
- [ ] Wits surgical antibiotic prophylaxis guideline
- [ ] Wits postoperative analgesia protocol
- [ ] Theatre safety checklist (WHO Surgical Safety Checklist)

---

## Junior Doctor

### Knowledge
- [ ] All intern competencies above
- [ ] Laparotomy indications and approach
- [ ] Hepatobiliary surgery principles
- [ ] Colorectal surgery principles
- [ ] Trauma surgery — ATLS principles, damage control surgery
- [ ] Vascular emergencies — AAA, acute limb ischaemia
- [ ] Surgical oncology principles — staging, margins, MDT

### Clinical Skills
- [ ] Chest drain insertion (supervised)
- [ ] Abscess incision and drainage
- [ ] Skin lesion excision (supervised)
