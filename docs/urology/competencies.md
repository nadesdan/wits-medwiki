# 💧 Urology Competencies

---

## Medical Student (Final Year)

### Knowledge
- [ ] Approach to haematuria — frank vs microscopic, causes
- [ ] Urinary tract infection — uncomplicated vs complicated, treatment
- [ ] Acute urinary retention — causes, management
- [ ] Benign prostatic hyperplasia — symptoms, assessment, management
- [ ] Renal colic — diagnosis, imaging, management

- [ ] Renal cell carcinoma — classic triad, investigation, management

### Knowledge
- [ ] All medical student competencies above

- [ ] Post-obstructive diuresis — recognition and management

- [ ] Male urethral catheterisation
- [ ] Female urethral catheterisation
- [ ] Bladder irrigation

---

!!! danger "Testicular torsion"
    Testicular torsion is a surgical emergency. Do not wait for ultrasound if clinical suspicion is high — time to surgery determines testicular salvage. Call urology immediately.
