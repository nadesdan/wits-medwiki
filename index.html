    🏥 Wits MedWiki¶

    Clinical knowledge for Wits medical students, interns, and junior doctors.

    Evidence-based summaries, clinical skills guides, and Wits-specific departmental protocols — structured using the Diataxis documentation framework.

    Content Framework¶

    Every article belongs to one of four types:

    🎓 Tutorial
    Learning-oriented
    Guides the learner through a topic step-by-step. Suitable for bedside teaching and self-directed learning.
    🔧 How-To Guide
    Task-oriented
    Helps a practitioner accomplish a specific procedure or clinical task. Assumes competence.
    📖 Reference
    Information-oriented
    Factual, authoritative, structured for quick lookup. Includes Wits protocols and drug charts.
    💡 Explanation
    Understanding-oriented
    Explains concepts, mechanisms, and context. Deepens clinical reasoning.
